About Chowking

Where Chinese and Filipino flavors come together

Our Story

Chowking was founded in 1985, bringing together two of the most loved cuisines—Chinese and Filipino. Our story began with a vision to make Chinese-style fast food more accessible to Filipinos through quick-service dining that combines flavor, convenience, and affordability.

We became part of Jollibee Foods Corporation in 2000, strengthening our operations and reach. Today, Chowking is a household name with hundreds of stores nationwide and even expanding internationally.

Our Mission

To serve great tasting Chinese food that everyone can enjoy, anytime and anywhere.

What Sets Us Apart

We serve a wide variety of Chinese dishes with a Filipino twist—from Chao Fan and Siomai to our signature Halo-Halo. What makes us stand out is our commitment to quality, affordable prices, and warm service that makes you feel at home.
